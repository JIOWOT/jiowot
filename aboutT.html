About Shaansun Technologies

We build connected systems combining embedded engineering, cloud platforms and AI analytics to solve real industry challenges.

Who We Are

Shaansun Technologies is an IoT company and training institute focused on delivering practical IoT products, end-to-end solutions and hands-on training programs to students, faculty and enterprises.

Mission

To empower industries and academia with accessible IoT solutions and practical training that accelerates technological adoption and innovation.

Vision

To be a leading force in regional IoT deployments and skill development — enabling smarter, safer and more sustainable systems.

Core Strengths

  • End-to-end IoT product development
  • Edge & cloud integration (MQTT, REST, WebSockets)
  • Low-power wide-area networking (LoRaWAN)
  • Sensor calibration & industry testing

Training

Hands-on workshops, bootcamps and semester-long courses taught by industry practitioners.

Future Roadmap

  1. Develop a plug-and-play IoT starter kit for colleges
  2. Launch a certification program in Industrial IoT
  3. Partner with municipalities for Smart City pilots
